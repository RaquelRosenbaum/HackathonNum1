Widget for Increasing Volunteer Enthusiasm

For placement on charity organization website to make it more likely that people thinking about volunteering will convert to active volunteers (increase conversion rate of website)
Making Volunteer Recruitment Easier, Faster, and most importantly, More Likely - Potential volunteers who are interested in an organization can see right on their webpage specifically what kinds of tasks the organization needs volunteer help with. The project is a widget for a charity organization’s webpage.
This way people will be more motivated to volunteer, when they see the tasks they like and have knowledge, skills or experience with. By making this visible, enthusiasm is built because it’s more concrete and in-the-present, not buried behind a excessive amount of time spent on onboarding.
Volunteers register by way of a form for their favorite types of tasks, one task on each card.
Registration is followed by a human screening phone call and intake process, which gives the volunteer token(s) for the tasks they are cleared to register for on a different page on the website. The volunteers can then go schedule themselves according to events and whether they have enough volunteers with the right skill tokens for each event. This project just goes as far as the steps prior to the human-implemented screening call/process.
How You Can Help Us Right Now As a Volunteer

On each card below, you will find specific ways we need help right now and the things you need to know how to do in order to help us in these tasks.
Click on each one that you are interested in doing, to learn more about it and/or register as a volunteer for that type of task.
You can register for as many task types as you would like.
After a short intake process with a representative who will reach out to you, you'll able to schedule yourself on our calendar page here on the website, for the events we have scheduled right now to do these tasks.
You'll be able to schedule as little as an hour or up to 30 hours per week in the tasks you are approved to work in.



Skill 1: Setting up chairs, tables, carrying and arranging objects up to 40 kilograms, standing for long periods of time

Skill 2: Packaging Food into Donation Boxes, requires being able to life up to 20 kilograms and standing for long periods of time

Skill 3: Assembling Sandwiches and other food items into meal boxes. Done while sitting, no lifting over 1 kilogram. Repetitive Motion.

Skill 4: Registering people at an event, using a tablet computer. Done while sitting, no lifting over 1 kilogram. Requires basic digital skills, ability to learn how to use software.

Skill 5: Doing fundraising outreach by telephone. Requires a background in sales or the ability to communicate well.

Skill 6: Driver for transporting freight. Requires commercial driver's license and clean record, vehicle is a small cargo van.

Skill 7: Driver for transporting passengers. Requires commercial driver's license for transporting up to 20 passengers and clean record, vehicle is a mini-bus.

Skill 8: Assisting the secretarial staff with various tasks. Done while sitting, requires ability to lift up to 3 kilograms. Requires ability to learn simple office tasks.

Skill 9: Helping the farmers harvest vegetables and fruits, able to lift up to 5 kilograms. Must be able to stand for at least 3 hours.

Skill 10: Helping the farmers harvest vegetables and fruits, able to lift up to 20 kilograms. Must be able to stand for at least 3 hours.

Skill 11: Helping to research and/or strategize lobbying efforts for organization. Background in political lobbying or law or some field of analysis required.

Skill 12: Teaching a class of participants with other staff members as part of a fun-day gathering. Topic is how to do a craft of your choice, like baking, paper mache, knitting, sewing, etc. Requires background in the handicraft being taught and some basic experience teaching.
